My Story


I am a full-stack web developer with a background in education.

I have nine years of experience as a teacher in Florida and New York. I have my Master's Degree and am very proud to have accomplished receiving a 4.0 GPA while working as the Graduate Assistant. During my career in education, I was the go-to person for solving a multitude of problems. I would help teachers and students with the math curriculum, biology, and language needs. I solved many challenges at my job, and problem solving, even technical edtech software issues, was my forte. More recently, I completed a tech bootcamp in Toronto at General Assembly at Bitmaker. It was an intensive fullstack web immersive course where I learned both front end and back end technologies. I recently booked a one-way flight to San Francisco to fullfill my dreams of breaking into tech and having the career of my dreams!
